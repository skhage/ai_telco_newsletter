This week in U.S. data technology, the industry is marked by aggressive **fiber expansion**, intensifying **fixed wireless competition**, and significant **M&A activity**—all driven by the race to capture broadband market share and deliver next-generation connectivity. Federal programs like BEAD^[Broadband Equity, Access, and Deployment] continue to accelerate fiber deployments, while telcos and cable operators are scaling up both wireless and FTTH^[Fiber to the Home] offerings[2](https://www.spglobal.com/market-intelligence/en/news-insights/articles/2025/10/moorgate-sees-fiber-demand-driving-m-a-among-large-midsize-operators-into-2026-94055375).

## Key Industry Trends

- **Fiber demand is driving major M&A**, with AT&T acquiring Lumen’s fiber assets and Charter acquiring Cox Enterprises’ broadband business[2](https://www.spglobal.com/market-intelligence/en/news-insights/articles/2025/10/moorgate-sees-fiber-demand-driving-m-a-among-large-midsize-operators-into-2026-94055375).
- **Fixed wireless adoption continues to surge**, led by T-Mobile, Verizon, and AT&T, now accounting for 15 million U.S. subscribers—up from nearly zero four years ago[2](https://www.spglobal.com/market-intelligence/en/news-insights/articles/2025/10/moorgate-sees-fiber-demand-driving-m-a-among-large-midsize-operators-into-2026-94055375).
- **Federal BEAD program** is reinforcing fiber investment, with two-thirds of $18.4 billion in awards targeting fiber builds for underserved areas[2](https://www.spglobal.com/market-intelligence/en/news-insights/articles/2025/10/moorgate-sees-fiber-demand-driving-m-a-among-large-midsize-operators-into-2026-94055375).
- **Layoffs and cost-cutting** are impacting major operators, reflecting competitive pressures and the need for operational efficiency[7](https://www.fierce-network.com/broadband/fierce-networks-2025-telecom-and-tech-layoff-tracker).

- **Fixed Wireless Growth:** AT&T is now a major player in fixed wireless, contributing to the rapid growth in this segment alongside T-Mobile and Verizon[2](https://www.spglobal.com/market-intelligence/en/news-insights/articles/2025/10/moorgate-sees-fiber-demand-driving-m-a-among-large-midsize-operators-into-2026-94055375).
- **Upcoming Earnings:** AT&T will report Q3 2025 results on October 22, with investors watching for updates on fiber and wireless strategy[6](https://finimize.com/content/t-asset-snapshot).
